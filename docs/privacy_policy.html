Privacy Policy

Applies to Time Alert ("the App"). Last updated: 2026-03-03

Information We Collect

  • Content you create (stored locally): alarms, timers, schedules, titles, and settings are stored on your device to provide reminder services.
  • Subscription and transaction data: Apple processes payments and provides subscription status. The App receives subscription status to unlock features and validate entitlements.
  • Device and network data (for subscription validation): subscription services (such as RevenueCat) may process device identifiers, IP address, app version, and related data to sync entitlement status and prevent fraud.

Note: The App does not require account creation and does not access your contacts, photos, or precise location.

How We Use Information

  • Provide alarm, timer, and schedule creation, storage, and reminders.
  • Verify subscriptions, sync entitlements, and restore purchases.
  • Maintain service reliability and security, including fraud prevention.

Sharing and Third-Party Services

  • Apple: Payments and subscriptions are handled by Apple. The App does not collect your payment details.
  • RevenueCat: Used for subscription status management and entitlement validation.

We do not share your personal information with other third parties except as required by law.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your reminder data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can delete alarms, timers, and schedules within the App.
  • Contact us for subscription assistance or privacy questions.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected such data, we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page, and material changes will be communicated where appropriate.
